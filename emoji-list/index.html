An emoji list refers to a collection list items with emoji as a bullet. Emoji's are small graphical icons or symbols used to convey emotions, ideas, or concepts in digital communication. Emojis are widely used in various forms of online communication, such as messaging apps, social media platforms, and emails, to add visual expressions and enhance the tone and meaning of text-based messages.

How to use

Add bling dependency into your FASTN.ftd
-- fastn.dependency: fastn-community.github.io/bling
Lang:
ftd
import emoji-list inside your .ftd file
-- import: fastn-community.github.io/bling/emoji-list
Lang:
ftd
copy any one of these -- emoji-list you like to use
-- emoji-list: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing

Properties:

Below are details of all properties of emoji-list component.
emoji
type:
String (required)
It's used to add emoji as a list bullet to emoji-list.
body
type:
Body (optional)
It's used to add body content to emoji-list component.
