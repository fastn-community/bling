Sidenote

A "sidenote component" is a UI component used to display additional or supplementary information related to the main content within a user interface.

Example

Sidenote:
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut

How to use

Add bling dependency into your FASTN.ftd
-- fastn.dependency: fastn-community.github.io/bling
Lang:
ftd
import sidenote inside your .ftd file
-- import: fastn-community.github.io/bling/sidenote
Lang:
ftd
sample usage of -- sidenote component
-- sidenote.sidenote:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor
incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis
nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut
Lang:
ftd

Properties:

body

Body (required)
